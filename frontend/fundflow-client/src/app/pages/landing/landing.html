Why finance teams switch to FundFlow

Built to make the audit the easy part.

FundFlow removes the three tasks that quietly consume a controller’s month — reconciliation, fund compliance, and error hunting.

⚡

Clear hundreds of transactions at once

Stop reconciling line by line. Match an entire bank statement and clear it in a single action, then move on to the work that actually needs your judgment.

Bulk clearing · up to 500 items per action
🔒

Restricted funds stay restricted

FundFlow segregates donor-restricted money automatically and enforces spending rules at posting time — not at year-end — so nothing slips across fund boundaries.

Compliance · enforced when you post, not at audit
🚨

Catch financial errors automatically

AI anomaly detection reviews every entry as it’s created, surfacing duplicates, outliers, and potential fraud the moment they appear — long before they reach a report.

AI detection · every entry scored in real time
